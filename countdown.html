How to Play

Welcome to the Countdown Game!

Objective: Be the player who clicks their button closest to when the countdown reaches zero without going over.

Rules:

  • Choose the number of players (2 to 10) and start the game.
  • A countdown timer will start and will disappear before reaching zero.
  • Press your player button as close as possible to when you think the timer reaches zero.
  • If you press after the timer reaches zero, you lose the round.
  • If only one player presses before time is up, they win the round by default.
  • Unpressed buttons will "explode" if not pressed before time runs out.
  • The game is played as a best of 5 rounds. The first player to win 3 rounds wins the game.
